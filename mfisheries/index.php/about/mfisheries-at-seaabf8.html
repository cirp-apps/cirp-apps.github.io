mFisheries @ Sea

 

 

The Suite Applications

  • Navigation

    Save and view locations at sea, view compass bearings and navigate to a desired location. View Alerts on a map and navigate directly to them.

  • Weather

    View information concerning current weather conditions and 3 day forecasts

  • Information

    Get First Aid information via audio, image and synchronized text; and audio stories of best practices for seafarers

  • Camera

    Capture moments at sea and upload to a personal map-based mFisheries Diary on the Web

  • Alerts

    Broadcast alerts to and receive alerts from fishermen within subscribed groups. View Alerts on a map and navigate directly to them.

  • SOS

    Communicate date-, time- and location-stamped SOS to local emergency response Authorities and pre-defined personal contacts
