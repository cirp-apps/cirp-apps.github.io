Understanding SOS

mFisheries SOS application icon can be found on the mFisheries home screen.
In the event of an emergency please press down on the SOS button to to launch the application. Select the "CONFIRM" button to initiate the SOS distress call.

 

 

 

After the SOS button has been pressed a timer will begin to count down. If you have selected the SOS button accidentally, you can cancel the call by tapping on the "CANCEL" button to the top of the screen.

 

 

Once the timer has counted down an SMS message will be sent to those on your emergency contact list, an email will be sent to the Trinidad and Tobago Coast Guard along with a pop up notification of your location.
